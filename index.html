Yan Huang

I am currently affiliated with the School of Foreign Languages at East China University of Science and Technology, where I also hold a position in the “Cognitive Neurolinguistics Laboratory” (ECUST), and am a member of the “Shanghai Key Laboratory of Brain-Machine Intelligence for Information Behavior” (SISU).

Recognized for my academic contributions, I have been honored as a recipient of the “Young Talent Cultivation Program” (Class A, 2022) and designated as “Researcher of the Year” (2023) by my academic institution.

My research expertise lies in the interdisciplinary fields of psycholinguistics, neurolinguistics, and second language acquisition, with a particular focus on the intricate relationship between language learning (Multilingualism) and cognitive development, as well as the exploration of individual differences in foreign language aptitude.

My research has been published in several prestigious international journals, including NeuroImage (SCI Q1 Top), Human Brain Mapping (SCI Q1), International Journal of Multilingualism (SSCI Q1), Journal of Multilingual and Multicultural Development (SSCI Q1), etc. Currently, I serve as a peer reviewer for several SCI/SSCI academic journals and am an international member of the American Psychological Association (APA).
